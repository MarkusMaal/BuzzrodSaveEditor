0x18 - 4 bytes year
0x1C - first half is day of month (1-15/16-31 dep. if the hour is odd or even), second half is month (1-15)
'0x1D - hour (divided by 2, remainder used to determine day of month)
0x1D - bits
	1: +2 to minute
	2: +1 to minute
	3: +16 to hour
	4: +8 to hour
	5: +4 to hour
	6: +2 to hour?
	7: +1 to hour
	8: Second half day of month
0x1E - minute by 4s
